Maxi's Sour Smite

2nd-level necromancy

Casting Time: 1 action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S, M (an acidic fruit)

Duration: Instantaneous

A special variant of inflict wounds - you make a melee spell attack against a creature you can reach in an attempt to steal a portion of their life. On a hit, the target takes 2d8 necrotic damage. If the target is already missing any of its hit points and isn't undead or a construct, you regain a number of hit points equal to half the amount of necrotic damage dealt + your spellcasting modifier.
