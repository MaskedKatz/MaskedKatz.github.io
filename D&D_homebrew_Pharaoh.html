Pharaoh Domain

Cleric Subclass

"Clerics of the Pharaoh Domain seek power and usually aim to be higher-up than those that they work with, even while looking up to deities. With deriving power from both the light and dark, they have the ability to drive one towards both life and death. Egyptian deities - such as Re-Horakhty, Anubis, and Bast - often promote such lifestyle and attitude. No matter the intention, the goals of these clerics can range from achieving fame and fortune, power, or even a following - for themselves or others - with the potential to protect those in need or raise an army to serve those who rule."
