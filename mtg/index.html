Here are all my Magic: The Gathering decks that I have built from scratch, in person. My decks are usually built for Legacy and are centered around a theme - using unpopular or older cards to create fun and creative mechanics. I may not have many decks, but that is because I put as much love as I can into each one.

Catastrophe/Cat Deck

White Mana

My very first deck! With the theme centering around some of my favorite things - cats and deserts - the gimmick is to overhelm your opponent by building up indestructible creatures with the help of Detainment Spell and Glittering Lynx/Lion - and wipe everything else away with Catastrophe (get it?) and Wrath of God.

Fling/Minotaur Deck

White/Red Mana

This deck focuses on buffing all your creatures with instants like Brute Force and Arrester's Zeal, and potentially double or triple (or even quadruple) damage from them using Fling and Insult//Injury to surprise your opponent or take them out of the game completely.
